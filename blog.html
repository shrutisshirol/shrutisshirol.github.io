Singularity 2016 & 2017 She has been the organizing core committee member of Singularity for the years 2016 and 2017. It is the prestigious science fest of IISER Bhopal. Different events that incorporated science and technology in a creative way to solve problems were organized and managed.
Social Initiative: PrayaasShe began as a team member in 2014, who volunteered to teach underpreviliged children. Later since 2016 until 2018 she has been the student representative of Prayaas. Under her leadership various events like women empowerment workshops for the underpreviliged, fund raising events for NGO, cleanliness drives in the village and cloth distribution drives were conducted.
Rashtriya Avishkar Abhiyaan (RAA) She has been a part of the government initiative undertook by IISER Bhopal, RAA, during 2016-2017 as a student leader along with Dr. Madhumita Mukherjee. She worked with a team on making syllabus that promotes critical thinking and unconventional ways to teach science and maths to students in underpriviliged government schools. She also went to schools to teach and interact every once in a week. Outreach activities were organized with the institute's help
Book Club She has been a co-ordinator and student representative of IISER Bhopal's book club from 2014 to 2017. She has worked on conducting events that could promote reading culture among the students and maintaing the club.
Hobbies & InterestsApart from the above, she enjoys reading, dancing and occasionally painting. She has been trained in Kathak (Indian Classical dance form) for 3.5 years. She is fond of running and has participated in mini-marathons held at IISER Bhopal, completing 4th among 40 participants. She has grown up reading mystery thriller by Sidney Sheldon and Enid Blyton. She is a huge fan of TV series such as Game of Thrones, The Big Bang Theory and F.R.I.E.N.D.S TV.
