Shruti was born in India. She is studying BS-MS (Physics) at Indian Institute of Science Education and Research (IISER), Bhopal. She is currently in her final year of studies and is pursuing master thesis at IISER, Bhopal and NTU, Singapore. She would be completing her BS-MS in May 2019 and aspires to do a Ph.D. Her interests have been in quantum computing research since the junior year and ever since she has constantly worked on exploring her interest. She has worked on model checking of quantum protocols, single electron spin qubits and is currently studying decoherence in macroscopic superposition states. Her next project is at NTU, Singapore in designing and characterizing superconducting quantum circuits. She has been awarded INSPIRE scholarship of Rs. 60,000 per year and entitled for an additional contigency of Rs. 20,000 for 5 years from 2014 to 2019 by the Department of Science and Technology, Government of India. She also has been a part of organizing team of Singularity, science fest of IISER Bhopal for two consecutive years. She has led the social initiative team of IISER Bhopal, 'Prayaas', to conduct workshops for underpreviliged women, raise funds for an NGO and cleanliness drives in the village. She was involved in a government scheme undertaken by IISER Bhopal to popularize quality scientific education in government schools.
